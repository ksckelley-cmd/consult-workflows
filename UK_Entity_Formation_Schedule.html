Situation

To establish a UK subsidiary in Edinburgh, with a target office-open window of September 1 – October 15, 2026, the UK Ltd must exist, be PAYE-registered, and have a signed employment contract before the Managing Director's permanent arrival.

Complication

Formation, HMRC registration, employment structure, and payroll are sequential dependencies with hard timing constraints in both directions. PAYE registration cannot occur more than 2 months before first payroll.

Question

What is the correct task sequence, ownership, timing, and cost to have a fully operational UK Ltd — with live PAYE and a filed Certificate of Coverage application — by October 1, 2026?

Answer — So What

Engage the Edinburgh accountancy firm on April 21 as the first action. The firm serves as registered office, payroll processor, HMRC correspondent, and CoC filer — single point of contact eliminating mail forwarding risk and provider fragmentation.

Standing Notes

1  ·  Engage the firm first — April 21

The firm is the registered office, payroll processor, HMRC correspondent, and CoC filer. Engaging them first compresses the schedule — they file incorporation the same week. Everything else flows through them. Do not incorporate before the firm is engaged.

2  ·  August 1 PAYE — hard date

The 2-month window is defined by the October 1 payroll date. Cannot register before August 1. Delegate execution to the firm; do not delegate awareness. Confirm in July that the firm has it calendared.

3  ·  Brief the firm at engagement, not later

This is not a standard single-entity UK payroll. The firm needs to understand the US parent relationship, split compensation structure, and CoC intent from day one. Provide the payroll comparison briefing and execution plan documents at the first meeting.

4  ·  York address — permanent director address

York is Kevin's director residential address (private, not on public register). Firm's Edinburgh address is the registered office for all company correspondence. Both remain in use for their respective purposes. Apply for Starling on the day of York entry.

5  ·  CoC — file October 1, expect gap

CA8421 filed on first payroll date. FICA withholding on US officer salary continues until issued certificate received (4–8 weeks). This is correct treatment — application alone provides no legal basis to stop withholding. Over-withheld FICA is recoverable at year-end.

6  ·  Six weeks of buffer — do not compress

Dry-run completes September 18. First live payroll October 1. The buffer absorbs payroll configuration delays, HMRC correspondence timing, and FX settlement questions with the firm. It exists for a reason.
